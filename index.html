About RDP

RDP Corporation is a Manufacturers' Representative and sales agency, specializing in instrumentation for measurement, calibration, sensing, data acquisition, testing, and analysis for the industrial, research, and scientific communities. RDP, an employee owned company, has been providing solutions to the most demanding technical issues since 1955. Offices are maintained throughout the area to support our customers in Ohio, Michigan, Indiana, Kentucky, West Virginia, and western Pennsylvania.
